Speak Up Cambridge

About Us

Are you paralyzed by the mere thought of standing in front of an audience, speaking up at a meeting, presenting your ideas or pitching your business concept?

At Speak Up Cambridge, we help members overcome public speaking anxiety through fast-paced, engaging meetups. We follow the Toastmasters International curriculum while adding our own variations. Meetings feature prepared speeches, impromptu speaking exercises, and a 30-second introduction round.

We meet on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of each month from 6:45–8:45 PM, with both in-person and online options available. Visitors can attend free as guests.
