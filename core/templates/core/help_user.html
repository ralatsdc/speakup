Member Guide

Welcome to SpeakUp, the Speak Up Cambridge Toastmasters club management app. This guide covers everything you can do as a club member.

Viewing Upcoming Meetings

The home page shows a list of upcoming meetings. Each meeting displays:

  • Date and meeting type
  • Theme and word of the day (if set)
  • Zoom link (if the meeting is hybrid or virtual)
  • A table of roles showing who is signed up for each one

Signing Up for a Role

To volunteer for a meeting role:

  1. Find the meeting on the home page.
  2. Look for roles marked as Open (no one assigned yet).
  3. Click the "Sign Up" button next to the role you want.

Your name will appear in the role assignment immediately — no page reload needed.

Good to know:

  • You can sign up for one role per meeting. (Officers may take additional roles.)
  • To drop a role you've already claimed, click the "Drop" button next to your assignment.
  • Guest accounts cannot sign up for roles. If you're listed as a guest and want to start taking roles, ask your club admin to update your account.

Adding Notes to Your Role

If you're assigned a role — especially a speech role — you can add notes that will appear on the public agenda (e.g., your speech title and Pathways project).

  1. Click the pencil icon next to your role.
  2. Type your notes in the text field that appears.
  3. Click Save.

Officers can edit notes on any role, not just their own.

Viewing the Meeting Agenda

Each meeting has a public agenda page that anyone can view (no login required). The agenda shows:

  • Meeting date, theme, and word of the day
  • All sessions and role assignments
  • Notes added by role holders
  • Zoom link (if applicable)

You can also download the agenda as a Word document using the download button on the agenda page. This is handy for printing or sharing.

Checking In at Meetings

At the meeting venue, a check-in kiosk is available (usually on a tablet near the entrance). No login is needed.

  • Members: Find your name in the grid and tap it to check in. Your button turns green to confirm.
  • Guests: Fill out the guest sign-in form with your name and email.

Emails You May Receive

The club admin may send you emails through the app:

  • Meeting reminders — Sent before a meeting. If you have a role, you'll get a reminder with your assignment details and a link to the agenda. If you don't have a role, you may receive a request to help fill open spots.
  • Post-meeting feedback — After a meeting, you may receive personalized feedback on your role from the meeting evaluators or officers.
  • Announcements — General club news and updates sent by the admin.

Password Reset

If you need to change your password:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Click "Forgot your password?"
  3. Enter your email address.
  4. Check your email for a reset link and follow the instructions to set a new password.
